Claim boundary

What the site will not imply.

ShardLoom is not presented as a production platform, an Apache Spark substitute, a broad SQL/DataFrame platform, an object-store/lakehouse runtime, a Foundry product, or a public performance winner.

No performance or superiority claim No Apache Spark substitute claim No production SQL/DataFrame claim No production object-store/lakehouse/Foundry claim No hidden fallback engine

Compute route

Front door is not the execution route.

A user can enter through Python, SQL, CLI, or benchmarks. ShardLoom still records the route through ingress, preparation, execution, output, and evidence.

01

Front door

Python, SQL, CLI, benchmarks, or an adapter express the work.

02

UniversalIngress

The source is admitted, classified, or blocked with a reason.

03

SourceState

Schema, fingerprint, adapter status, and source evidence become reusable state.

04

vortex_ingest

Admitted non-Vortex data is prepared into VortexPreparedState.

05

Execution

prepared_vortex, native_vortex, certified cold route, direct one-shot, or generated source.

06

OutputPlan

Result, local sink, Vortex artifact, or future platform sink is planned separately.

07

Evidence

Certificates, no-fallback fields, materialization boundaries, timing, and claim gate.
