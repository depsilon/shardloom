Unsupported Diagnostics

Unsupported diagnostics are deterministic reports for paths ShardLoom does not currently execute.

In this dossier

Why it matters How ShardLoom uses it What it proves Claim boundary Source docs

Why it matters

They are part of the no-fallback contract: unsupported work should be clear, stable, and actionable.

How ShardLoom uses it

Capability views, phase items, and report-only rows expose blockers instead of silently invoking another engine.

What it proves

A blocked path can be intentionally documented without pretending it is implemented.

What it does not prove

A report-only row is not runtime support.

Evidence fields

  • support_status
  • unsupported_reason
  • required_evidence
  • fallback_attempted

Claim boundary

Unsupported/report-only explanations reduce overclaiming risk but do not create new runtime support.
