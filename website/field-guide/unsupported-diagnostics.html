Plain-English meaning

Unsupported diagnostics are deterministic reports for paths ShardLoom does not currently execute.

Why it matters

This concept helps users interpret ShardLoom's evidence and claims posture without reading the phase plan or RFCs first.

How ShardLoom uses it

ShardLoom surfaces this through docs, capability/status pages, use cases, or evidence fields such as support_status, unsupported_reason, required_evidence, fallback_attempted.

Current support

Current status is current-diagnostic-pattern. Treat that status exactly as shown; scoped, smoke, report-only, planned, blocked, or unsupported posture must not be read as broader runtime support.

Evidence fields

It helps identify the exact evidence or documentation surface that applies to this concept.

  • unsupported_reason
  • required_evidence
  • fallback_attempted

Claim boundary

What it does not claim

Unsupported/report-only explanations reduce overclaiming risk but do not create new runtime support.
