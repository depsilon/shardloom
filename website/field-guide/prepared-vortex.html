Prepared Vortex

Prepared Vortex is the current runtime-development lane for already prepared Vortex artifacts.

In this dossier

Why it matters How ShardLoom uses it What it proves Claim boundary Source docs

Why it matters

It removes compatibility import from the timed path and makes prepared/native optimization work easier to interpret.

How ShardLoom uses it

Prepared rows expose source-backed scan, Native I/O, materialization/decode, no-fallback, and claim-gate fields.

What it proves

A prepared row can show scoped local execution over prepared Vortex artifacts with explicit evidence fields.

What it does not prove

It does not prove generalized encoded aggregation, production SQL/DataFrame runtime, or superiority over other engines.

Evidence fields

  • prepared_vortex
  • source_backed_scan_*
  • native_io_certificate_status
  • materialization_boundary_report_emitted

Claim boundary

Prepared Vortex is the main optimization direction, but current rows remain claim-gated.
