Plain-English meaning

License and provenance checks keep dependency licensing, SBOMs, checksums, attestations, and copied-code risks visible before publication.

Why it matters

This concept helps users interpret ShardLoom's release and trust posture without reading the phase plan or RFCs first.

How ShardLoom uses it

ShardLoom surfaces this through docs, capability/status pages, use cases, or evidence fields such as sbom_status, checksum_manifest_status, provenance_status, fallback_dependency_absent.

Current support

Current status is current-gate. Treat that status exactly as shown; scoped, smoke, report-only, planned, blocked, or unsupported posture must not be read as broader runtime support.
