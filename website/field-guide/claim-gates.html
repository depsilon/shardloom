Claim Gates

Claim gates state whether a row is claim-grade, fixture-smoke, report-only, blocked, unsupported, or not claim-grade.

In this dossier

Why it matters How ShardLoom uses it What it proves Claim boundary Source docs

Why it matters

They keep public interpretation tied to available correctness, benchmark, certificate, and policy evidence.

How ShardLoom uses it

Website, benchmark, and CLI outputs preserve `claim_gate_status` and explicit not-claimable posture.

What it proves

A row can say which claim boundary applies to the current evidence.

What it does not prove

A claim gate label does not expand support beyond the named workload and evidence refs.

Evidence fields

  • claim_gate_status
  • performance_claim_allowed
  • claim_boundary
  • support_status

Claim boundary

If evidence is missing, public claims stay closed.
