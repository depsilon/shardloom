Interpretation rule

Cold and warm rows mean different things.

Certified cold rows include ingest/stage/certification work. Prepared warm rows start after VortexPreparedState exists. External engines provide local baseline context only and never satisfy ShardLoom evidence gates.

Cold route

Certified cold ingest/stage

Compatibility import rows include ingress, parse, Vortex ingest, write/reopen, scan, result sink, and evidence work.

Warm route

Prepared warm query

Prepared/native rows are the runtime-development direction after Vortex preparation exists.

Baseline only

External baselines

Pandas, Polars, DuckDB, DataFusion, Dask, and Spark rows are baseline context, not ShardLoom evidence gates.
