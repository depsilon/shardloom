First 10 minutes local smoke

PowerShell

What you get

Status, smoke, and capabilities JSON with fallback_attempted=false and external_engine_invoked=false.

Evidence fields

  • fallback_attempted=false
  • external_engine_invoked=false
  • protocol_version
  • resolved_cli_path
